# Memory Garden - How to Use

> Quick start guide for everyday use.

---

## 1. First launch (Onboarding)
1. Open the app and tap through the welcome screens.
2. Choose a visual style for your garden.
3. **Plant your first memory** - quickly tap a mood or a suggested memory card. The app creates your first memory for you to confirm with one tap, **with no typing required**.
4. Watch your first memory **bloom into a rare plant** (guaranteed for the first one), then take the short tour.
5. At first, the app only shows the core features. As you complete the first few goals, more features unlock gradually like in a game.

### Progress-based unlocks
- After **Goal 1**: you see Garden, simplified Create, Atlas preview, and reduced Settings.
- After **Goal 2**: full Atlas core, recent milestone cues, and **Quick-capture** unlock.
- After **Goal 3**: Timeline, Share, Backup, and more support features unlock.

## 2. Add a memory
1. Tap the **+ (Add)** button on the home/garden screen.
2. Add a **photo**, a short **description**, pick a **category** and **tags**, and set your **mood**.
3. Save. In a hurry? Use **Quick-capture** to save just 1 photo + 1 line.
4. Your memory instantly grows a new plant in the garden.

## 3. Grow your garden
- Each memory plants a seed that grows through stages: **Seed -> Sprout -> Plant -> Bloom -> Rare Bloom**.
- Keep adding memories to fill your garden with more plants and flowers.
- During its matching **season**, a plant glows and has a higher chance of a rare bloom.

## 4. Collect plants (Atlas / Pokedex)
1. Open the **Collection / Atlas** tab.
2. Early on, Atlas appears as a lighter preview; after more goals, it expands into the full collection screen.
3. Unlocked plants show their art and **dex number**; locked ones show a silhouette.
4. Aim to fill every empty slot and complete each category.

## 5. Explore your timeline
- Switch between **Year**, **Month**, and **Life Journey** views to relive your memories in order.
- This tab unlocks after **Goal 3**, once you have enough memories and the app can safely show more complexity.

## 6. Earn milestones
- Reach goals like *First Memory*, *10 Memories*, *First Trip* to unlock badges.

## 7. Add a widget
- Long-press your home screen -> Widgets -> Memory Garden. Choose **Recent Memory**, **Garden Progress**, or **On This Day**.

## 8. Share a memory
- Open a memory (or your garden) -> **Share** -> a styled Memory Card image is created for social media.
- Sharing is unlocked after **Goal 3**.

## 9. Back up your data
- Go to **Settings -> Backup -> Export ZIP** to save a copy. Use **Import ZIP** to restore.
- The app reminds you when it's time to back up.
- Optional: upload the ZIP to your own Google Drive.
- Backup opens later in the journey so the early experience stays focused on planting memories first.

## Tips
- Switch hemisphere in **Settings** if you're in the Southern Hemisphere (flips the seasons).
- Watch a **Rewarded Ad** to claim a Mystery Seed, Growth Boost, or an out-of-season seed.
