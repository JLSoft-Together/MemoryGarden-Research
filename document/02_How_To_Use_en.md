# Memory Garden — How to Use

> Quick start guide for everyday use.

---

## 1. First launch (Onboarding)
1. Open the app and tap through the welcome screens.
2. Choose a visual style for your garden.
3. **Plant your first memory** — pick a photo from your gallery. The app auto-fills the date, location, and a suggested category. Confirm with one tap.
4. Watch your first memory **bloom into a rare plant** (guaranteed for the first one), then take the short tour.

## 2. Add a memory
1. Tap the **＋ (Add)** button on the home/garden screen.
2. Add a **photo**, a short **description**, pick a **category** and **tags**, and set your **mood**.
3. Save. In a hurry? Use **Quick-capture** to save just 1 photo + 1 line.
4. Your memory instantly grows a new plant in the garden.

## 3. Grow your garden
- Each memory plants a seed that grows over time: **Seed → Sprout → Plant → Bloom → Rare Bloom**.
- Keep adding memories to fill your garden with more plants and flowers.
- During its matching **season**, a plant glows and has a higher chance of a rare bloom.

## 4. Collect plants (Atlas / Pokédex)
1. Open the **Collection / Atlas** tab.
2. Unlocked plants show their art and **dex number**; locked ones show a silhouette.
3. Aim to fill every empty slot and complete each category.

## 5. Explore your timeline
- Switch between **Year**, **Month**, and **Life Journey** views to relive your memories in order.

## 6. Earn milestones
- Reach goals like *First Memory*, *10 Memories*, *First Trip* to unlock badges.

## 7. Add a widget
- Long-press your home screen → Widgets → Memory Garden. Choose **Recent Memory**, **Garden Progress**, or **On This Day**.

## 8. Share a memory
- Open a memory (or your garden) → **Share** → a styled Memory Card image is created for social media.

## 9. Back up your data
- Go to **Settings → Backup → Export ZIP** to save a copy. Use **Import ZIP** to restore. The app reminds you when it's time to back up. (Optional: upload the ZIP to your own Google Drive.)

## Tips
- Switch hemisphere in **Settings** if you're in the Southern Hemisphere (flips the seasons).
- Watch a **Rewarded Ad** to claim a Mystery Seed, Growth Boost, or an out-of-season seed.
